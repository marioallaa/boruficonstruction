FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of construction projects does Borufi Construction specialize in?

Borufi Construction specializes in a wide range of construction projects, including renovations, extensions, new builds, and more. Our expertise spans residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, ensuring quality craftsmanship and exceptional results for every project.

How long does it typically take for Borufi Construction to complete a project?
    The timeline for completing a project depends on various factors such as size, complexity, and scope. On average, Borufi Construction completes projects within 8 to 12 weeks. However, timelines may vary based on specific project requirements and unforeseen circumstances.
What measures does Borufi Construction take to ensure safety on construction sites?
    At Borufi Construction, safety is our top priority. We adhere to strict safety protocols and regulations to create a secure working environment for our team and clients. Our personnel are trained in safety procedures, and we conduct regular inspections and assessments to mitigate risks and prevent accidents.
Does Borufi Construction provide warranties for its construction work?
    Yes, Borufi Construction stands behind the quality of our workmanship and materials. We offer warranties on our construction projects to provide peace of mind to our clients. The duration and coverage of warranties may vary depending on the type of project and specific contractual agreements.
