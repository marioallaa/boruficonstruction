Health & Safety

At Borufi Construction, the health and safety of our employees is our top priority. We are committed to creating a safe and healthy work environment by implementing effective health and safety practices and procedures. Our approach to health and safety is based on the "See, Think, Act" framework, empowering our employees to identify hazards, assess risks, and take proactive measures to prevent accidents and injuries.

1. See

Hazard Identification:
All employees are responsible for actively identifying hazards in their work environment. This includes hazards related to construction materials, equipment, tools, and site conditions.

Regular Inspections: 
We conduct regular inspections of our construction sites to identify potential hazards and ensure compliance with health and safety regulations. Employees are encouraged to report any hazards they observe to their supervisors immediately.

2. Think

Risk Assessment:
Before beginning any task, employees are required to assess the risks associated with the task and implement appropriate control measures to mitigate those risks.

Safety Training: 
We provide comprehensive safety training to all employees to ensure they are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to work safely. This includes training on proper equipment usage, emergency procedures, and hazard recognition.

3. Act

Safety Procedures:
Employees are expected to follow established safety procedures and protocols at all times. This includes wearing appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE), using equipment and tools correctly, and adhering to safe work practices.

Reporting Incidents: 
In the event of an accident, injury, or near-miss, employees are required to report the incident to their supervisor immediately. We investigate all incidents thoroughly to identify root causes and implement corrective actions to prevent recurrence.


We are committed to continually improving our health and safety performance through regular review, evaluation, and feedback. We encourage open communication between management and employees to identify areas for improvement and implement effective solutions.
At Borufi Construction, we believe that everyone plays a crucial role in maintaining a safe and healthy work environment. By adhering to the principles of "See, Think, Act," we can work together to prevent accidents, protect our employees, and promote a culture of safety throughout our organization.
