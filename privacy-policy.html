Privacy Policy

1 . Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Borufi Construction, a construction company based in London, collects, uses, maintains, and protects personal information obtained from users ("Users") of its website ("Site") and related services. By using the Site, Users consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection

Personal Data: 
Borufi Construction may collect personal data from Users when they visit the Site, register, fill out forms, or interact with the Site's features. This may include but is not limited to, name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and other information voluntarily provided by Users.

Non-personal Data: 
In addition to personal data, Borufi Construction may collect non-personal data such as browser information, device type, IP address, and website usage statistics.


3. Use of Information

Borufi Construction may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve services offered through the Site.
  • To personalize User experience and communicate with Users.
  • To fulfil requests and respond to inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activities.


4. GDPR Compliance

Borufi Construction is committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant data protection laws. 

This includes:

  • Providing Users with transparent information about data processing activities.
  • Obtaining explicit consent for processing personal data, where required.
  • Implementing appropriate security measures to protect User data.
  • Honouring Users' rights to access, rectify, and erase personal data.


5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Borufi Construction does not sell, trade, or rent Users' personal data to third parties. However, personal data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers for the purposes of website management, analytics, or other services related to the operation of the Site. Borufi Construction may also disclose personal data if required by law or to protect its rights or property.


6. Data Retention

Borufi Construction will retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.


7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data and request information about its processing.
  • Rectify inaccuracies in their personal data.
  • Erase personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of their personal data.
  • Receive a copy of their personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Users can exercise these rights by contacting Borufi Construction using the contact information provided below.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Borufi Construction reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Continued use of the Site after the posting of changes constitutes acceptance of those changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or Borufi Builders Limited, do not hesitate to contact us.
